About Us

We design and write academic content for use in schools and organizations around the world. We are more than the standard instructional & design company. We work to improve the quality of academic content and its digital delivery to enhance the learning experience and its outcomes for learners and the organizations/institutions they represent. We are looking for writers, thinkers, designers and subject matter experts in most disciplines to help us create a better way to learn. Bring your brain to Edufic and share it with the world. You can help improve learning for millions of Learners, including yourself. With so much at stake in education, the Edufic admittance process has become rigorous. Only the best candidates are accepted. Our acceptance standards and rates are more aggressive than those of the best schools in the world because we are creating academic content for students at those schools and for employers and organizations who demand a very high level of skill and performance from their team members. Therefore we need the Edufic team to be the best communicators, have high-level comprehension and critical thinking skills, and be confident in their subject knowledge.
